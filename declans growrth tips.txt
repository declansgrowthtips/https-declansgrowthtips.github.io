<section id="how-to-grow" class="how-to-grow">
    <h2>How to Grow</h2>
    <p>Growing taller involves a combination of genetics, diet, exercise, and lifestyle habits. Here are some steps you can take:</p>
    <ol>
        <li>Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, calcium, and vitamins.</li>
        <li>Stay active with sports, stretching, and exercises that promote growth.</li>
        <li>Ensure you get 8-10 hours of sleep per night, as growth hormones are released during sleep.</li>
        <li>Avoid substances that can stunt growth, such as tobacco and alcohol.</li>
    </ol>
